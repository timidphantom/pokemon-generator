ABOUT & FAQ

About

PokéGenerator is a fanmade Pokémon project designed to quickly show game information about certain Pokémon. It also works as a tool to randomize the available Pokémon with just the press of a button!

PokéGenerator is powered by PokéAPI.

Frequently Asked Questions

PokéGenerator uses a random number generator (or your own search!) to pull the information of a random Pokémon from PokéAPI.

Unfortunately, some Pokémon with different forms don't display correctly when searching up its name -- for example, Basculin which has both its Red Stripe and Blue Stripe form. Instead, you can search up its National Pokédex number and it'll return one form of the Pokémon you're looking for. Additionally, Pokémon added in the Hisui and Paldea regions are not included yet.

The base stat total displays your Pokémon's strengths and weaknesses. There are 6 statistics that a Pokémon might excel or struggle in, and this tool displays each Pokemon's base stat total.

Pokémon are creatures which inhabit the world of an eponymous video game and television series, created by the game company GameFreak. Each Pokémon has their own types, strengths, and weaknesses, which is what this tool displays.
